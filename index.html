Spiderman

The Friendly Neighborhood Superhero

Origin

Peter Parker was bitten by a radioactive spider, giving him super strength, agility, and the ability to cling to walls.

Motto

“With great power comes great responsibility” — this one line from uncle ben is the reason peter became spiderman

Fun Fact

Spiderman first appeared in *Amazing Fantasy #15* in 1962, created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko.
